MT. OLIVES ACADEMY

Mt.Olives Academy was started by the Magodi area community back in 2013 as Magodi A.I.C primary school
as a centre to teach Christian values and provide basic education to young boys and girls.
Starting with eight (8) pupils in 2013,
the school has witnessed a continous growth in population currently having 156 Enrolled Students.
Most of the pupils are either orphans or from a poor family background.

For more information visit us

Our School's Location

MT. OLIVES ACADEMY is located at Magodi Market 19 kms off Likoni-Lunga Lunga Highway
